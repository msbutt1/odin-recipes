Iced Tea


Description

This iced tea recipe makes a fabulous chilled drink and tastes just like the brand name versions! Use your favorite good-quality black tea and as much sugar and lemon juice as you like for the perfect balance.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water
  • 3 orange pekoe tea bags
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large saucepan. Remove from heat and add tea bags. Cover and steep for 1 hour.
  2. Combine tea and sugar in a large pitcher. Stir until sugar dissolves; stir in lemon juice. Refrigerate until chilled.
